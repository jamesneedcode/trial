Barbell curls

Is a classic bicep exercise where you hold a barbell with an underhand grip and curl it up towards your chest.

how to do it

dumbbell curls

Similar to barbell curls, but using dumbbells instead. You can vary the angle of your grip (neutral, underhand, or overhand) to target different parts of your biceps.

how to do it

Hammer curls

This variation involves holding the dumbbells with a neutral grip (palms facing each other) and curling them up towards your shoulders. This targets the brachialis muscle, which lies underneath the biceps.

how to do it

Concentration curls

This is an isolation exercise where you sit on a bench with one arm braced against your inner thigh and curl a dumbbell towards your shoulder. This helps to target the peak of the biceps.

how to do it

Preacher curls

This is a machine-based exercise where you rest your arms on a pad and curl the weight towards your shoulders. This helps to isolate the biceps and reduce swinging or cheating.

how to do it

Cable curls

This involves using a cable machine and a variety of attachments (e.g., straight bar, rope, etc.) to perform curls at different angles and levels of resistance.

how to do it

Military press

Also known as a shoulder press, this exercise is a classic shoulder workout. It works the entire shoulder muscle, including the front, side, and rear deltoids.

how to do it

Arnold press

This exercise is a variation of the shoulder press and is named after Arnold Schwarzenegger. It targets the front and side delts and also engages the triceps.

how to do it

Front raise

This exercise primarily targets the side deltoids and helps to increase shoulder width. You can perform this exercise with dumbbells, cables, or resistance bands.

how to do it

Lateral raise

This exercise primarily targets the side deltoids and helps to increase shoulder width. You can perform this exercise with dumbbells, cables, or resistance bands.

how to do it

Rear delt fly

: This exercise targets the rear deltoids and can be performed with dumbbells or a cable machine.

how to do it

Upright row

This exercise targets the upper traps and the side deltoids. It can be performed with a barbell, dumbbells, or a cable machine.
